Corn and Cheese

If I were to give this photoblog a name, I'll call it just that.

It's an analogy. To remind myself that life is too short not to appreciate every second we have; the awkward, the uncertain, the corny and the cheesy moments of life. And with that, an appreciation, a mantra to live by; to be the person you've always wanted to be and aspire to become.

A bit about myself. I was born and have spent most of my childhood in the Philippines , before our family emigrated to the middle of Middle Earth – Wellington, New Zealand . Having finished high school there, I then moved north to study Bachelor of Commerce at The University of Auckland.

After graduating, I've since moved to Sydney, Australia to work in technology within a financial services firm – which is where I'm currently at today.
