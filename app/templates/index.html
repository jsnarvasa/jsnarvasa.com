About Me

I am an aspiring data scientist, graduating with a Business Degree in Information Systems and Operations Management during my undergraduate at The University of Auckland, and somehow finding myself with a passion for optimisation and simulation models along with coding by the end of it.

During the day, I work within the financial services industry, looking after the trading applications used by the Fixed Income trading desk, and by night, currently pursuing my postgraduate in Data Science at The University of Sydney.

A knack for angles and a believer of composing for a purpose, I quite like the idea of telling a story through photography. I’m also interested in people’s listening habits, and how one’s behaviour affects their music choice. Both above, spawning side-project ideas which I’ve worked on in my spare time.
