I AM JESSE S NARVASA.

Hi, I’m Jesse and welcome to the place where I convey my story through photography. As the expression goes “a picture is worth a thousand words” and that every photo tells a story; thus, the idea of creating this photo blog was born with the intention of not having to write words that I normally would in a blog.

A little about myself, I work within technology at a financial services firm after graduating with an Information Systems and Operations Management degree at The University of Auckland.

Why did I make this site? Well, I wrote this site for two reasons, the first being my first attempt I made whilst at university just wasn’t cutting it anymore and I wanted the challenge in writing a dynamic site from scratch + the fun that comes with it; the second reason is that I’ve just got a whole lot of photos from my camera which I don’t want to just sit in my computer.
