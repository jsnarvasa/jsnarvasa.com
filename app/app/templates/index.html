I AM JESSE S NARVASA.

Corn and Cheese

If I were to give this photoblog a name, I'll call it just that.

It's an analogy; to remind myself that life is too short not to appreciate every second we have: the awkward, the uncertain, the corny and the cheesy moments of life. And with that appreciation, a mantra to live by; to be the person you've always wanted be and aspire to become.

A little about myself. Now in Sydney, I work within technology at a financial services firm after graduating from my Bachelor of Commerce degree at The University of Auckland.

What's this for? You may ask. Right now, it's the home of my photos accumulated over time since I saved enough money to buy my DSLR back in first year of university. Later on, perhaps much more than that, including the timelapse pieces which originally got me into photography.

For now, I'll take it one step at a time.
